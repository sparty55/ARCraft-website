About US

ARCraft - a 3D educational AR game to develop children's spatial reasoning abilities

Who we are?

We are ARCraft team from USC Games.


What we did in this project?

Inspired by Minecraft Earth, we try to design an educational AR game, using Unity, Xcode, AR foundation and spatial anchors. Via building structures, villages, and even cities in the AR world, 5-15 year olds can develop their spatial reasoning abilities. That is to say, our game can serve as a gamified engineering training simulator with a decent incentive system, which motivates our target audience for future study in STEM fields.


What tools are we using?

-Unity

-AR Foundation

-Xcode

-Spatial Anchors

Current Progress

  • Menu main to select different levels.
  • Tutorial to understand the game operations.
  • Grid system to fix block coordinates.
  • Preview object to help player know the target for each level.
  • Block rotation, accurate stack placement, drag and drop inventory system to build complex structure.
  • Transparency of the material selection to minimize UI affection to AR work space.
  • Grading system to evaluate blocks' position and direction.
  • Light effect system to simulate a real environment from sunrise to sunset.
  • Summary panel to understand success or failure.

Game Concept

The player would enter a tutorial to get familiar with the game operation. After the player accomplished the tutorial and level selection, a preview of a specific object will be shown to players in each level, and players are required to construct the target object based on this preview with a given amount of blocks in a limited time. Player’s construction operation will be conducted on a highlighted platform in the actual camera scene. Once the remaining block number or time becomes 0, our game will evaluate players’ work based on the remaining time and the similarity of the constructed structure and the target structure.


Target Audience

5-15 year olds

Genres

3D, Educational, AR

Objective

To challenge more levels with more objects, the player needs to get a certain score in the previous level construction.
